About Sweet Crumbs Bakery

Sweet Crumbs Bakery is a local bakery that specializes in freshly baked goods made from high-quality ingredients. The bakery offers a variety of products including cookies, muffins, breads, and cupcakes that are prepared daily for customers.

This inventory management system allows Sweet Crumbs Bakery to track ingredients and finished baked products efficiently. By managing parts and products, the bakery can ensure accurate inventory levels and maintain consistent product availability.
